Horus Heresy

After the unknown fall of Humanity, the last great remnants of Earth set out to reconquer the stars. Led by eighteen brothers and thier father; upon its greatest victory the best and brightest is chosen to lead in his fathers stead. However, moments away from restoring humanity the newly elected Warmaster rebels. Setting into motion a never ending war.Strife continued for the next eight years in the traitors favor who through a numerous means recruited half of his brothers to rebel. His efforts destroyed the a majority of the work they originally set out to do. The war turned upon where it began, Earth with the traitors laying siege against the scattered remains of the loyalist factions. Though victory seemed certain, the traitors fell upon one another or set out for their own glory. At the peak of the fight, the emperor set out to quell the best of his sons.
